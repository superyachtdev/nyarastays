About the home

A jungle sanctuary, two terraces, two pools.

Penthouse by Nyara is one of the most quietly desired homes in Tulum — a three-bedroom penthouse set into the canopy of Aldea Zama. Total comfort for nine. Two private terraces, each with its own pool. A barbecue for the long evenings. And, on every side, the green hush of the Riviera Maya jungle.

Inside, the language is warm and modern: travertine floors, double-height ceilings, woven pendant lights, deep teak joinery. Floor-to-ceiling glass folds the living and dining rooms straight out onto the water. The kitchens are compact but complete — espresso, a stocked larder, everything you need for a slow morning in.

It is private, calm and luxurious — the kind of place guests return to. Self check-in by lockbox, a quiet street, and a privileged position inside Aldea Zama that keeps the whole of Tulum within easy reach.

The neighbourhood

A short ride to the best of Tulum.

Aldea Zama sits between Tulum town and the beach road — ten minutes either way. The cenotes of the inland jungle, the ruins above the sea, the long white sand of the hotel zone, and the cafés and mezcalerías of the centro: all of it within a short, easy reach of your door.
